Why did I choose Anzu?

Anzu is a small, local, authentic karaage restauraunt that serves well priced, excellent, and delicious food. I have been going to Anzu for years now as a person who loves trying the diverse range of restauraunts that surround my dream school of UCLA in Sawtelle.

I always want to show my support as much as possible to local spots like this and I chose to make my project orient around Anzu as I wanted to improve the functionality of their current website, while maintaining the casual and stylish character the restauraunt works hard to maintain.

I plan on supporting more businesses like Anzu as I visit LA more and more.

The Best Karaage in Sawtelle

Casual, Delicious, Karaage

Looking for a laid-back spot to indulge in some mouth-watering Japanese karaage in LA?

Look no further than our joint! Our chicken is made with the finest ingredients and cooked to crispy, golden perfection, giving you the perfect balance of texture and taste. We've got a range of dipping sauces that are made in-house to complement your chicken perfectly.

Whether you're dining in or taking out, our friendly staff will make sure you're taken care of. So why not treat yourself to a plate of our delicious karaage and a cold drink? You won't regret it!
